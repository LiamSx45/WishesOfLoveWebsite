Wishes of Love

A moving story about adoption, family, and love

Liam Sawyer details his adoption story and helps reinforce the theme that DNA does not make a family, but rather love.

He starts his story from as early as he could remember, in a vast mountainous range all the way to the elaborate environment that is New York City.

He tells of the mysterious and strange foreigners that came into his orphanage and whisked him and his older sister, who played a significant mother figure in his early childhood, away to start a brand-new life.

His journey tells of that of the unexpected and how nothing but change and new things were brought into his life from there on out.

About the author

Liam Sawyer is a published author with his first book,
Wishes of Love: A Memoir of Love, Hope, and Family which was published mid-2020.

Liam Sawyer is a published author, hobbyist photographer, and loves to create new things such as graphic art, apps, and new clothing designs. He founded Sawyer Original in 2020 right after he published his first book Wishes of Love.

He currently attends Manhattan College for a B.S. in Computer Engineering with a Minor in Mathematics. Being the sole founder of Sawyer Original, he is constantly trying to improve the user experience for everyone who decides to visit.

Liam Sawyer

About the Author

Liam Sawyer is a published author, hobbyist photographer, and loves to create new things such as graphic art, apps, and new clothing designs. He founded Sawyer Original in 2020 right after he published his first book Wishes of Love.

He currently attends Manhattan College for a B.S. in Computer Engineering with a Minor in Mathematics.

Being the sole founder of Sawyer Original, he is constantly trying to improve the user experience for everyone who decides to visit.

More info about Liam Sawyer

2021


As of 2021, Liam Sawyer has started his Podcast called Life in Focus and has continued his blog titled Sawyer Talks. He has also released two sticker applications on the Apple App Store that are free to download and use. He continues to update his photography with new and improved skills.

2020


In April, Liam Sawyer published his first book titled Wishes of Love as a college assignment for his English class. Following this book, Liam Sawyer began his blog titled Sawyer Talks.

2019


In June, Liam Sawyer graduated high school from Xaverian High School, located in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. At the beginning of September, Liam Sawyer started college at Manhattan College which is located in Riverdale, Bronx. He began his college career as a Computer Science major before switching to Electrical/Computer Engineering.

2005


Liam Sawyer was adopted by his parents, June and William. They adopted not only Liam but his sister Caitlyn also and since 2005, Liam has lived in Staten Island, New York.

2000


Liam Sawyer was born in a small village in Ukraine, located in the Kirovohrad Oblast. He lived there for a few years until he was sent to an orphanage.
